About Me

Originally from Auburn, Washington I now live in Fort Worth, Texas and attend Texas Christian University. Being a student at TCU was one of the best decisions I could have ever made. Not only is the sunny weather nice compared to the many rainy days growing up in Washington, but the oppurtunities and friendships I have made I am incredibly grateful for.

I am an English and Writing double major. If you would have told me I would be graduating with a degree in English back in high school, I probably would have laughed because it used to be my worst subject. That's exactly why it grew on me because I had to work really hard to be decent at writing, then I slowly fell in love with it. Now, you can find my short stories and poems published in TCU's Journal of the Arts, eleven40seven.

Interests

Growing up I was always creative and my mom was gracious enough to support my random endeavors. Meaning, I would beg her to take me to Hobby Lobby so I could buy more paints or a needle and thread. I've grown to love art. During the weekends, you can find me volunteering as an exibition host at Amon G. Carter Musuem.
